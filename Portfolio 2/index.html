bluepeace -
non profit organization

The website I will create is for a non-profit organization that is called Bluepeace. Bluepeace is an organization that was created with the purpose of saving and helping sea life everywhere. The purpose of the website that will be created for Bluepeace is to make it easy for people worldwide to get information about the cause and just learn more about the problem.
People are going to be able to read information about Bluepeace and see how they are able to help out in any possible way. There will be information about how people can contribute with money or just with their time. The target audience will mostly be adults and people who are concerned about sea life in general and just wants to help out.

photo story -
mobile application for iOS

People now a day like taking pictures and sharing them to family and friends. The application we have created can make a slideshow with as many pictures you like of your year for example. That is something social media is going towards we have seen.
Our application can be used to visually track the progress of different things in life by taking a photo each day and then combine all the photos into a video. The main purpose of the application is, for example, you want to take a selfie every day for a one-hole year and make a slide show of all the pictures. So, our application does is put all the pictures automatically into a slide show and you can decide timestamp, the interval of time every picture is shown in the slideshow and music in the background.
